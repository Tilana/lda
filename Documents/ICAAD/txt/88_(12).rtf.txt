IN THE HIGH COURT OF FIJI
AT SUVA
CRIMINAL JURISDICTION

HIGH COURT CRIMINAL CASE NO: HAC 022 OF 2009

BETWEEN:

STATE
PROSECUTION

AND:

MOHAMMED MUSTAFA HAKIM
ACCUSED PERSON

Counsel: State - Ms. T. Leweni
Accused Person - Mr I. Khan

JUDGMENT OF THE COURT

The three Assessors have given their unanimous opinions that the accused is not guilty as charged in Count No. 1 and guilty as charged in Count No. 2.

I direct myself in accordance with my summing up and I agree with them.

Therefore I find the accused not guilty on Count No.1 and acquit the accused on Count No. 1.

I find the accused guilty on Count No. 2 and convict him on Count No. 2.

Priyantha Fernando
Puisne Judge
