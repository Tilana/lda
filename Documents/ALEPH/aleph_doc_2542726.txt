<h2 align="center"><b>ROYAL STANDARD MINERALS INC. HAS SECURED A CREDIT FACILITY
AND
COMPLETES A PRIVATE PLACEMENT FINANCING </h2>
<p>C.U.S.I.P. # 780919106 <br>
LISTED: STANDARD &amp; POORS <br>
OTCBB:RYSMF </p>
<h4>MANHATTAN, NEVADA, SEPTEMBER 7, 2010, ROYAL STANDARD MINERALS INC., </h4>
</b></font>
<p><font size="+1" color="#000000">announces today that it has secured a 
financing, with
an international private investment group and current management of the Company 
to include
a &quot;Credit Facility&quot; that will consist of senior secured notes with an 
8% per
annum interest rate and warrants for $10-$20 million, representing 20-40 units 
at $500,000
per unit. Also, the funding includes 500,000 warrants per unit priced @ $0.10 
per warrant.
The term of the notes and warrants will be three years and include interest only
payments
during the first year of the notes commencing six months after the closing date.
During
years 2 and 3 the repayment of the notes will include equal monthly principal 
and interest
payments until the notes are repaid. The notes will be secured by RSM properties
and
physical assets. The documentation and release of funds for the initial funding 
of the
credit facility is expected to be completed on or about October 15, 2010. </p>
<p>In conjunction with the credit facility as part of a separate transaction the
Company
has closed a $1.2 million private placement financing that includes 11.2 million
units at
$0.10/ unit. Each unit will consist of one common share and one common share 
purchase
warrant priced at $0.10 per warrant of the Company. Each Warrant is exercisable 
into one
common share in the capital of the Company (a &quot;Common Share&quot;) for a 
period of
three years from the date of the closing. <b></p>
<p></b>The use of proceeds will be to continue with the development of RSM's 
Nevada
gold-silver properties and for working capital purposes. The Company may be 
subject to a
structuring fee in connection with this financing. </p>
<p>RSM is a natural resource exploration and development company,
www.royalstandardminerals.com </p>
<p>For further information about this release contact Mr. Roland Larsen, (775) 
487-2454
and/or Mr. Rich Kaiser, Investor Relations, 800-631-8127. <b></p>

press

prices, product
demand, market competition, imprecision of mineral estimates, and other risks 
detailed
herein and from time to time in the Securities and Exchange Commission filings 
of the
Company. </strong></p>
