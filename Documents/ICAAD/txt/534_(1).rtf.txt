JUDGMENT

The majority opinion of the assessors is that the 1st, the 2nd, the 3rd, the 5th and the 6th accused are guilty of the offence with which they have been charged. I direct myself in accordance with my summing up. I agree with the opinions of the assessors and I convict the 1st, 2nd, 3rd, 5th and 6th accused accordingly.

The unanimous verdict of the assessors is that the 4th accused is not guilty of the charge. Again I direct myself in accordance with my summing up and I acquit him.

Nazhat Shameem
JUDGE

At Suva
