Information  on  the  Resources  in  this  release  is  based  on  an  independent  evaluation  conducted  by  RISC  Operations  Pty  Ltd  (RISC),  a  leading  independent  
petroleum  advisory  firm.  The  evaluation  was  carried  out  by  RISC  under  the  supervision  of  Mr Peter  Stephenson,  RISC  Partner,  in  accordance  with  the  SPE-­
