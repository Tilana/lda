<P STYLE="font: 11pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0 0 12pt; text-
align: justify">As described in a prior news release

<P STYLE="font: 11pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0 0 2pt; text-align:
center"><B>DynaResource de M&eacute;xico, S.A.
de C.V. awarded Damages of $ 48 M USD</B></P>
<P STYLE="font: 11pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0 0 6pt; text-align:

<P STYLE="font: 11pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0 0 6pt; text-align:
justify"><B>Mazatl&aacute;n, Sinaloa M&eacute;xico
(October 12, 2015) </B>DynaResource de M&eacute;xico SA de C.V. 
(&#8220;DynaM&eacute;xico&#8221;, and &#8220;the Company&#8221;),
the 100% owner of the San Jose de Gracia Project, located in the County of 
Sinaloa de Leyva, State of Sinaloa, M&eacute;xico (&#8220;SJG&#8221;,
and the &#8220;SJG Project&#8221;), announces that it was awarded damages in 
excess of <B>$48 M USD (Forty-Eight Million Dollars)</B>
on October 05, 2015, by virtue of a sentence issued by the Thirty Sixth Civil 
Court of the Superior Court of Justice of the Federal
District of M&eacute;xico (Tribunal Superior de Justicia del Distrito Federal), 
File number 1120/2014. Additional Resolutions were
also ordered in favor of DynaM&eacute;xico (the Damages, and the additional 
Resolutions are together referred to as, the &#8220;Oct.
5, 2015 Resolution&#8221;).</P>
