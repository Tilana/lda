<P STYLE="font: bold 24pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin-right: 0; margin-
left: 0">Sandstorm Gold Files Early Warning Report</P>
<P STYLE="font: 12pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin-right: 0; margin-left:
0">VANCOUVER, Aug. 12, 2015 /CNW/ - Sandstorm
Gold Ltd. (&quot;Sandstorm&quot; or the &quot;Company&quot;) (NYSE MKT: SAND, 
TSX: SSL) is announcing, pursuant to National Instrument
62-103 - <I>The Early Warning System and Related Take Over Bid and Insider 
Reporting Issues</I>, the purchase by Sandstorm of an
aggregate of 2,362,401 common shares (the &quot;Acquired Shares&quot;) of AuRico
Metals Inc. (&quot;AuRico&quot;), representing
approximately 2% of the outstanding common shares of AuRico (the &quot;AuRico 
Shares&quot;).</P>
<P STYLE="font: 12pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin-right: 0; margin-left:
0">Prior to the acquisition of the Acquired Shares,
Sandstorm owned directly an aggregate of 11,812,001 AuRico Shares, which 
represented approximately 10% of the issued and outstanding
AuRico Shares.&nbsp; Upon completion of the acquisition of the Acquired Shares, 
an aggregate 14,174,402 AuRico Shares are now owned
directly by Sandstorm, representing approximately 12% of the issued and 
outstanding AuRico Shares.</P>
<P STYLE="font: 12pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin-right: 0; margin-left:
0">The Acquired Shares were acquired by Sandstorm
on the open market over the facilities of the Toronto Stock Exchange and other 
published markets for the AuRico Shares at prices
ranging from $0.51 to $0.55 per Acquired Share. The acquisition of the Common 
Shares by Sandstorm was effected for investment purposes.
Sandstorm may from time to time acquire additional securities of AuRico, dispose
of some or all of the existing or additional securities
it holds or will hold, or may continue to hold its current position.</P>
<P STYLE="font: 12pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin-right: 0; margin-left:
0">The early warning report, as required under
National Instrument 62-103, contains additional information with respect to the 
foregoing matters and will be filed by the Company
on AuRico's SEDAR profile at www.sedar.com.</P>
<P STYLE="font: 12pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin-right: 0; margin-left:
0"><B>ABOUT SANDSTORM GOLD</B></P>
<P STYLE="font: 12pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin-right: 0; margin-left:
0">Sandstorm Gold Ltd. is a gold streaming and
royalty company. Sandstorm provides upfront financing to gold mining companies 
that are looking for capital and in return, receives
the right to a percentage of the gold produced from a mine, for the life of the 
mine. Sandstorm has acquired a portfolio of 72
streams and royalties, of which 14 of the underlying mines are producing. 
Sandstorm plans to grow and diversify its low cost production
profile through the acquisition of additional gold streams and royalties.</P>
<P STYLE="font: 12pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin-right: 0; margin-left:
0">For more information visit: www.sandstormgold.com.</P>
