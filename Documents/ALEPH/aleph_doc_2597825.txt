Posting of Annual Report & Notice of AGM
Ilika (AIM: IKA), a pioneer in materials innovation and solid-state battery technology, confirms that
the Annual Report and Accounts for the year ended 30 April 2016 and the Notice of the Annual
General Meeting will be posted to shareholders today. Copies are available on the Company's
website: www.ilika.com/investors
Ilika's Annual General Meeting will be held at 2pm on Thursday, 29 September 2016 at the
offices of Eversheds LLP, One Wood Street, London EC2V 7WS.
