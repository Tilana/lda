<P STYLE="font: 10pt/normal Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-
indent: 0.5in">On October 29, 2014
(&ldquo;Closing Date&rdquo;), Rocky Mountain Minerals Corp. (&ldquo;RMM&rdquo;) 
the 100% owned subsidiary of Bullfrog Gold
Corp. (the &ldquo;Company&rdquo;) entered into an Option Agreement
(&ldquo;Option&rdquo;) with Mojave Gold Mining Corporation
(&ldquo;Mojave&rdquo;). Mojave holds and possesses the purchase rights to 100% 
of 12 patented mining claims
(&ldquo;Property&rdquo;) located in Nye County, Nevada. This Property is located
adjacent to the Company&rsquo;s Bullfrog
Gold Project in Nevada. This Property is strategically located adjacent to the 
Company&rsquo;s Bullfrog Gold Project in
Nevada and covers the NE half of the Montgomery-Shoshone open pit that was mined
by Barrick Gold Corp. from the
mid-1980&rsquo;s to late 1990&rsquo;s.</P>
<P STYLE="font: 10pt/normal Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-
indent: 0.5in"></P>
<P STYLE="font: 10pt/normal Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-
indent: 0.5in">&nbsp;</P>
<P STYLE="font: 10pt/normal Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-
indent: 0.5in">Mojave granted to RMM the sole and immediate
working right and option with respect to the Property, for the period from the 
Closing Date of the Option until the 10<SUP>th</SUP>
anniversary of the Closing Date, to earn a One Hundred Percent (100%) interest 
in and to the Property free and clear of all charges
encumbrances and claims, save and except for the Royalty (as defined in the 
Option attached as Exhibit 10.1).</P>
<P STYLE="font: 10pt/normal Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-
indent: 0.5in">&nbsp;</P>
<P STYLE="font: 10pt/normal Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-
indent: 0.5in">In order to maintain in force the working
right and Option granted to it, and to exercise the Option, Rocky Mountain 
granted Mojave 750,000 common shares and paid $16,000.
RMM must pay to Mojave a total of $190,000 over the next 10 years.</P>
<P STYLE="font: 10pt/normal Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-
indent: 0.5in">&nbsp;</P>
<P STYLE="font: 10pt/normal Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-
indent: 0.5in">The foregoing is not a complete summary

complete text of the Option attached as Exhibits
