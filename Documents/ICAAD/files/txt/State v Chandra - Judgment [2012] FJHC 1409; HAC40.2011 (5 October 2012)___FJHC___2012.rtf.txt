JUDGMENT

[1]     
Suresh Chandra, you have been charged with the following offence:

Statement of Offence

MURDER: Contrary to section 237 of the Crimes Decree No. 44 of 2009.

Particulars of Offence

SURESH CHANDRA on the 15th day of January, 2011 at Nasinu in the Central Division murdered Farzana Begum.

[2]     
In the majority opinion of three assessors you have been found guilty of the charge. The case as prosecuted was a largely circumstantial case, and as such the circumstantial evidence against you was strong.

[3]     
I agree with the majority verdict of the assessors and find you guilty as charged. You are convicted accordingly. That is the judgment of the Court.

Paul K. Madigan
