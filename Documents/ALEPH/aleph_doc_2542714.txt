COAST OIL TRUST (NYSE&#151;ROYT) (the &#147;Trust&#148;) a perpetual royalty

today a cash distribution to the holders of its units of beneficial interest of 
$0.08808 per unit, payable on November&nbsp;14, 2014, to unitholders of record 
on November&nbsp;5, 2014. The Trust&#146;s distribution relates to net profits 
and overriding royalties generated during September&nbsp;2014 as provided in the
conveyance of net profits and overriding royalty interest.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" 
style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" 
style="font-size:10.0pt;">This month&#146;s distribution of $3.5 million is 4% 
lower than the previous month ($0.08808 per unit vs. $0.09219 per unit) 
principally due to lower average realized oil prices, partially offset by lower 
lease operating expenses. Average realized prices for the Developed and 
Remaining Properties were $84.32 per Boe, as compared to $88.82 per Boe in 
August, or 5% lower.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" 
style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" 
style="font-size:10.0pt;">The current net profits amount from the Developed 
Properties was approximately $3.4 million. The current month&#146;s 
l</font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">ease operating expenses for the
Developed Properties, including property taxes, were $3.9 million compared to 
$4.2 million in the prior month reflecting lower workover expenses. The current 
month&#146;s capital expenditures for the Developed Properties were in-line with
the prior month at $0.6 million. The current month&#146;s distribution also 
includes $0.1 million for the 7.5% overriding royalty on the Remaining 
Properties from 37 Orcutt Diatomite wells, six Orcutt Field wells, four East 
Coyote wells and one West Pico well. The cumulative deficit of the net profits 
interest on the Remaining Properties, including the 7.5% overriding royalty 
payments, stands at $2.2 million compared to $2.3 million in the prior 
month.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" 
style="font-size:10.0pt;">Trust administrative expenses and the monthly 
operating and services fee payable to PCEC totaled approximately $0.2 million 
and were deducted in the calculation of the total distribution.</font></p>
