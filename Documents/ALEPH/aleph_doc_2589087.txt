<P STYLE="font: bold 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0 0 0 5pt; 
text-align: center">Quantum Materials Corp Developing
Nanomaterials for Tracing Fracking Fluids to Point of Origin</P>
<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: 
justify; text-indent: 0.5in">&nbsp;</P>
<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: 
justify">San Marcos, TX &ndash; August 3, 2016 &ndash;
Leading North American quantum dot manufacturer <U>Quantum Materials 
Corporation</U> (OTCQB:<U>QTMM</U>) today announced that the
Company has partnered with a Texas-based E&amp;P technology business and is 
currently developing specialized nanomaterials for
use in oil and gas well production optimization. The project, which is nearing 
the completion of the initial phase, is targeting
new materials which are expected to provide for safer and more efficient 
recovery of hydrocarbon resources.</P>
<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: 
justify; text-indent: 0.5in">&nbsp;</P>
<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: 
justify">&ldquo;We are excited to be a part of this
project which has the potential to bring a wealth of new information to the oil 
and gas industry.&rdquo; said Sri Peruvemba, CEO
of Quantum Materials Corp. &ldquo;However more importantly to Quantum, the 
challenges of creating nanoparticles that can withstand
the harsh downhole environment are leading us to discoveries that we can apply 
to make our quantum dots for use in displays and
lighting more robust.&rdquo;</P>
<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: 
justify; text-indent: 0.5in">&nbsp;</P>
<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: 
justify">The Company realized a small revenue contribution
from this project in fiscal year 2016 that ended June 30.</P>
<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align:
justify; text-indent: 0.5in">&nbsp;</P>
<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: 
justify"><U>About Quantum Materials Corp</U></P>
<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: 
justify; text-indent: 0.5in">&nbsp;</P>
<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: 
justify">Quantum Materials Corporation (QMC) develops
and manufactures quantum dots and nanomaterials for use in medical, display, 
solar energy and lighting applications through its
patented volume production process. QMC&rsquo;s volume manufacturing methods 
enable consistent quality and scalable cost reductions
to drive innovative discovery to commercial success. The company&rsquo;s 
technology continues to move the future of quantum dots
to the present. QMC&rsquo;s products are the foundation for technologically 
superior, energy efficient and environmentally sound
LCD UHD displays, the next generation of solid-state lighting, solar 
photovoltaic power applications, advanced battery and energy
storage solutions, biotech imaging, and biomedical theranostics. Wholly-owned 
subsidiary <U>Solterra Renewable Technologies</U>
develops sustainable quantum dot solar technology.</P>
