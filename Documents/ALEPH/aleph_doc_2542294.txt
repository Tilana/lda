NEWS RELEASE                                                    NOVEMBER 4, 2005
CORPORATE UPDATE
VANCOUVER,  CANADA - ROCHESTER  RESOURCES LTD. (TSXV: RCT AND OTCBB:  RCTFF) Mr.
Des O'Kell,  President,  is pleased to provide this further  update on corporate
activities.
In October  the Company  announced  that it had  initiated  due  diligence  on a
property of merit which met the Company's criteria for near term production. The
project is a  silver/gold  project  located in  Mexico.  The  results of the due
diligence  conducted  to date  indicate  that the property  meets the  Company's
criteria  for near  term  production  and  also  holds  significant  exploration
potential.  The  Company  has  initiated  negotiations  on terms to  acquire  an
interest in this project.
Rochester Resources Ltd.,  incorporated in British Columbia,  Canada,  trades on
the TSX Venture  Exchange under the symbol "RCT",  and on the OTCBB under symbol
"RCTFF".  The Company's directors are experienced in the resource sector and are
focused on enhancing  shareholder value by expanding  Rochester's assets in this
sector.  Management  aims to identify  exploration  projects of high merit,  and
quickly  complete  confirmation  exploration.  This will  enable the  Company to
confirm the potential of a project and reach advanced project status by the most
cost effective means and in the shortest time possible.
