<P STYLE="font: 10pt/normal Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-

<P STYLE="font: 10pt/normal Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-
align: justify">Rimrock Gold Corp. (the &ldquo;Registrant&rdquo;)
was unable, without unreasonable effort or expense, to file its Quarterly Report
on Form 10-Q for the period ended May 31, 2015
(the &ldquo;Quarterly Report&rdquo;) by the July 15, 2015 filing date applicable
to smaller reporting companies due to a delay
experienced by the Registrant in completing its financial statements and other 
disclosures in the Quarterly Report.&nbsp;As a result,
the Registrant is still in the process of compiling required information to 
complete the Quarterly Report and its independent registered
public accounting firm requires additional time to complete its review of the 
financial statements for the period ended May 31,
2015 to be incorporated in the Quarterly Report.&nbsp;The Registrant anticipates
that it will file the Quarterly Report no later
than the fifth calendar day following the prescribed filing date.&nbsp;</P>
