<P align="center"> <B>PART III &#151; NARRATIVE</B> </P>
<P align="justify">State below in reasonable detail why Forms 10-K, 20-F, 11-K, 
10-Q,10-D, N-SAR, N-CSR, or the transition report or portion thereof, could not 
be filed within the prescribed time period.</P>
<P style="margin-left:10%;margin-right:10%;" align="justify"><b>Management of 
Royal Mines and Minerals Corp. (the "Company") was unable to obtain the business
information necessary to complete the preparation of the Company's audited 
financial statements for the year ended April 30, 2015 and the review of these 
financial statements by the Company's auditors in time for filing. Such 
information is required in order to prepare a complete filing. As a result of 
this delay, the Company is unable to file its Annual Report on Form 10-K within 
the prescribed time period without unreasonable effort or expense. The Company 
expects to file within the extension period.</b></P>
