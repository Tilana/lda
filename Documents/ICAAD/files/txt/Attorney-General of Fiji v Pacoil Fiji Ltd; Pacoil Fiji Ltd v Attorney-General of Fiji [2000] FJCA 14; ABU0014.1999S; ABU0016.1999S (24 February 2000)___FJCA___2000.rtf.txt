DECISION OF THE COURT
 
The abovenamed appellants seek leave to appeal to the Supreme Court against the decisions of this Court given on 29 November 1996 and 7 January 2000. We are satisfied that the questions raised are of significant public importance and certify accordingly in terms of s.122 (2) (a) of the Constitution. 

Leave to appeal is granted.
