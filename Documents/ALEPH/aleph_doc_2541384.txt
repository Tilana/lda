BG Group Trading Update - for the period 1 October to 30 November 2005
Following strong production performance and favourable trading conditions, BG
Group is today updating the market with the information set out below:
As anticipated at the Q3 2005 results, upstream production has increased
significantly in the fourth quarter. Aggregate production for October and
November was approximately 35 mmboe.
These volumes have been produced in a firm price environment with average
realised prices of approximately:
Oil price $59 per barrel
Liquids price $44 per barrel
UK gas price 36p per therm (contracted and uncontracted)
International gas price 21p per therm
During the same period, BG Group's LNG activities have continued to perform well
with five LNG trains in production and increased activity in marketed cargoes,
against a backdrop of firm US gas prices.
