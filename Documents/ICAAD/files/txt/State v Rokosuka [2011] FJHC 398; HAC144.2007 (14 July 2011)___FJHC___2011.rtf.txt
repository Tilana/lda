JUDGMENT

[1] Kitione Rokosuka, you are charged with the following offence:

Statement of Offence

MANSLAUGHTER: Contrary to section 198, 201 and 213 of Penal Code, Cap. 17.

Particulars of Offence

SUREN CHAND s/o Hans Raj and KITIONE ROKOSUKA on the 9th of August 2006 at the Western Division, by an unlawful omission that is, failing in their duty to take reasonable precautions in securing a live electric wire, caused the death of LUKE JOHN MOLNAR.

[2] After being directed by me on the law the three assessors have found you guilty of the offence by a majority of two to one. 

[3] Having directed myself on my own Summing Up I must agree with the majority and I find you guilty as charged and I convict you of the offence.

Paul K. Madigan
JUDGE

At Lautoka
14 July 2011
