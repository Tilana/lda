IN THE HIGH COURT OF FIJI
AT SUVA
CRIMINAL JURISDICTION

Criminal Case No: HAC049 of 2009

BETWEEN:

THE STATE

AND:

JEREMAIA WAQA

Counsel: Mr. J. Singh for State
Ms T. Leweni for Accused

Date of Hearing: 17th June 2009
Date of Sentence: 17th June 2009

SENTENCE

[1]     
The accused is convicted on his plea of guilty to a charge of possession of an illicit drug. On 8 January 2007 the accused was found in possession of 0.8 grams of Cannabis.

[2]     
He has two previous convictions which are 9 years old. I disregard those convictions and treat the accused as a person of previous good character.

[3]     
The accused is married with a child. He is the sole breadwinner for his family. He earns $1000 per month as a marine engineer.

[4]     
The amount of Cannabis involved is relatively small. It was neither for personal use nor for sale. The Cannabis was given to him by a friend.

[5]     
The accused is remorseful for his conduct. He pleaded guilty at the first reasonable opportunity. He has spent 12 days in remand.

[6]     
Taking these matters into account, the accused is sentenced to a fine of $100.00 in default 3 months imprisonment.

[7]     
The fine must be paid within 7 days.

[8]     
Drugs to be destroyed within 14 days.

Daniel Goundar
JUDGE

At Suva
17th June 2009
