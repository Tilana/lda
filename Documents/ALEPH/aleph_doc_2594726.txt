EXPLANATORY NOTE
This Registration Statement on Form S-8 is filed to register an additional 8,000,000 Common
Shares of Goldcorp Inc. (the “Registrant”) under the Goldcorp Inc. Restricted Share Plan, which
are the same class as those Common Shares registered under the Registrant’s Registration
Statements on Form S-8 filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission (the “Commission”)
on June 22, 2005 (File No. 333-126040), May 29, 2008 (File No. 333-151251), May 3, 2012 (File
No. 333-181116) and May 8, 2014 (File No. 333-195816). This Registration Statement has been
prepared in accordance with General Instruction E of Form S-8 and the previously filed
Registration Statements are incorporated by reference in this Registration Statement, except to
the extent supplemented, amended or superseded by the information set forth herein.
