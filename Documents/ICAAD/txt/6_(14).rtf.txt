Mr. G.P. Lala for the Respondent

Date of Orders: Friday, 24 May 2002

MINUTE OF ORDERS

1. Note that the respondent withdraws his notice of motion filed on 14 May 2002.

2. Appeal brought by the appellants dismissed.

3. No order as to the costs of the respondents motion.

4. The costs of the appeal to abide the outcome of the appeal to the Supreme Court.

5. The appellants application for leave to appeal to the Supreme Court is granted. The questions raised by the grounds stated in the notice of appeal are certified to be questions of significant public importance.

6. Liberty to any party to apply.

Sheppard JA, Presiding Judge
Tompkins, JA
Smellie, JA

Office of the Attorney General Chambers, Suva for the Appellants
