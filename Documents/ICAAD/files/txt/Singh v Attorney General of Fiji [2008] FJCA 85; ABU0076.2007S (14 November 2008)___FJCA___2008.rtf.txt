JUDGMENT OF THE COURT

[1]     
The appeal in this matter came on for hearing before us on 6th November 2008. At the end of the hearing Dr Sahu Khan, counsel for the appellant, indicated that he was likely to receive instructions from his client to discontinue his appeal.

[2]     
In the circumstances we adjourned any further hearing of the matter until those anticipated instructions were received.

[3]     
On 10 November 2008 the appellants solicitors filed a Notice of Discontinuance, duly signed and consented to by the respondent, there to be no order as to costs.

[4]     
In the circumstances, we order that:

(1)     
The appeal be dismissed.
(2)     
The orders made by Winter J on 31 October 2007 be confirmed.
(3)     
No order as to costs.
