New Base Salaries
     On December 5, 2005, the Committee approved a 4 percent increase in the annual base
salary for the Company’s executive officers. The Committee based its decision on various
factors, including compensation studies which include salary and bonus compensation data from
several competitor companies including certain of those companies contained within the S&P
500 Oil and Gas Drilling Index. The base salaries for the executive officers effective January 1,
2006 are as follows:

Consulting Arrangement
     As announced by the Company on December 8, 2005, George S. Dotson, following his
retirement on March 1, 2006, will provide advisory services to the Company for a period of at
least one year and will continue to serve as a Director of the Company until the expiration of his
current term in March 2007. In consideration of such advisory services, Mr. Dotson shall be paid
a monthly fee of $25,000.
