IN THE HIGH COURT OF FIJI
AT LAUTOKA
CIVIL JURISDICTION

ACTION NO. HBM0007 OF 2005L
(Ba Matrimonial Case No. 26 of 1996)

BETWEEN:

TIMOCI NASAVULAGI
Appellant

AND:

FANNY VEROHIKA SMITH
Respondent

Counsel for the Appellant: Ms. N. Khan for Mr. G.P. Shankar
Respondent in Person

ORDER

The appellant granted bail to appear at High Court Lautoka on the 9th May 2005 at 10.00am subject to the following conditions:

1.      
Surety in sum of $200.00.

2.      
Pay the sum of $500.00 a release in reduction of the arrears of maintenance.

3.      
Surrender all travel documents.

4.      
To reside at Tavarau, Ba.

5.      
That Bail be entered at Ba Magistrates Court and payment be made at that court.

Order the Ba Magistrates Court to furnish the Court Record on or before the 5th May 2005.

JOHN CONNORS
JUDGE
At Lautoka
22 April 2005
