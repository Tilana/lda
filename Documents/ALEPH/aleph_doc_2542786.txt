PART III — NARRATIVE
State below in reasonable detail why Forms 10-K, 20-F, 11-K, 10-Q,10-D, N-SAR, N-CSR, or the
transition report or portion thereof, could not be filed within the prescribed time period.
Management  of  Ireland  Inc.  (the  "Company")  was  unable  to  obtain  the  business
information  necessary  to  complete  the  preparation  of  the  Company's  interim  financial
statements for the three and nine months ended September 30, 2014 and the review of
these financial statements by the Company's auditors in time for filing. Such information is
required in order to prepare a complete filing. As a result of this delay, the Company is
unable to file its Quarterly Report on Form 10-Q within the prescribed time period without
unreasonable effort or expense. The Company expects to file within the extension period.
