<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: center; MARGIN: 0in 0pt 0pt" align=center><B><FONT 
style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt" lang=EN-US color=windowtext face="petrobras sans,sans-
serif">RNEST processing record in July</FONT></B></P>
<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: center; MARGIN: 0in 0pt 0pt" align=center><B><FONT 
style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt" lang=EN-US color=windowtext face="petrobras sans,sans-
serif"></FONT></B>&nbsp;</P>
<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: center; MARGIN: 0in 0pt 0pt" align=center><B><FONT 
style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt" lang=EN-US color=windowtext face="petrobras sans,sans-
serif"></FONT></B>&nbsp;</P>
<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify; MARGIN: 0in 0pt 0pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 
12pt" lang=EN-US color=windowtext face="petrobras sans,sans-serif">Rio de 
Janeiro, August 17, 2016 &#8211; Petr&#243;leo Brasileiro S.A. &#8211; Petrobras
hereby announces that the Abreu e Lima Refinery (RNEST) beat its processed 
feedstock record in July 2016, reaching 99.18 thousand barrels per day (bpd), 
2.3% more than the previous record achieved in June (96.96 thousand bpd). In 
total, 3.07 million barrels were processed, 4.6% up on the May 2016 record of
2.94 million barrels. This was the third consecutive monthly record of the 
Refinery, which is located in the Suape Port Industrial Complex in Ipojuca, 
Pernambuco.</FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify; MARGIN: 0in 0pt 0pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 
12pt" lang=EN-US color=windowtext face="petrobras sans,sans-
serif"></FONT>&nbsp;</P>
<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify; MARGIN: 0in 0pt 0pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 
12pt" lang=EN-US color=windowtext face="petrobras sans,sans-serif">The 
Refinery&#8217;s oil product output in July was also its best ever, with Diesel 
S-10 production of 373.57 thousand m&#179;, the country&#8217;s biggest figure, 
corresponding to 32% of national output of this product, and 1.7% more than the 
previous record in March 2016 (367.39 thousand m&#179;). Production of 
petrochemical naphtha also reached the record level of 46.64 thousand tons, 1.6%
up on May 2016.</FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify; MARGIN: 0in 0pt 0pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 
12pt" lang=EN-US color=windowtext face="petrobras sans,sans-
serif"></FONT>&nbsp;</P>
<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify; MARGIN: 0in 0pt 0pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 
12pt" lang=EN-US color=windowtext face="petrobras sans,sans-serif">In addition, 
operational changes and project modifications have been increasing the 
profitability of Petrobras&#8217; Brazilian refineries, with the generation of 
an additional US$ 355 million in revenues through 2015, an estimated US$ 572 
million accumulated until the end of 2016 and an estimated US$ 759 million until
2019. The initiatives are part of the Intermediate Products and Gasoline 
Production Program (Promega) and based on capital discipline, focus on 
profitability and strengthened performance management.</FONT></P>
