<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="3" face="Arial" style="font-
size:12.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Suncor Energy to acquire interest in North Sea 
Rosebank project</font></b></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Arial" style="font-
size:11.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Arial" style="font-
size:11.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Calgary, Alberta (Aug.&nbsp;9, 2016) 
</font></b><font size="2" face="Arial" style="font-size:11.0pt;">&#150; Suncor 
Energy UK Limited (&#147;Suncor&#148;) today announced it will acquire a 30 per 
cent participating interest in the U.K. North Sea Rosebank project</font><font 
size="2" face="Arial" style="font-size:11.0pt;"> from OMV (U.K.) 
Limited.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Arial" style="font-
size:11.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Arial" style="font-
size:11.0pt;">Under the terms of the agreement, Suncor will make an initial 
payment of US$50 million on closing. </font><font size="2" face="Arial" 
style="font-size:11.0pt;">The transaction is subject to conditions, including 
regulatory approval, and is anticipated to close in the fourth quarter of 
2016.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Arial" style="font-
size:11.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Arial" style="font-
size:11.0pt;">In the event the co-venturers approve the Rosebank project final 
investment decision and Suncor elects to participate, Suncor could pay 
additional consideration to OMV (U.K.) Limited of up to US$165 million, subject 
to adjustments according to the terms of the agreement.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Arial" style="font-
size:11.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Arial" style="font-
size:11.0pt;">The Rosebank project, located approximately 80 miles (130 
kilometres) northwest of the Shetland Islands</font><font size="2" face="Arial" 
style="font-size:11.0pt;"> in water depths of approximately 3,600 feet (1,110 
metres), </font><font size="2" face="Arial" style="font-size:11.0pt;">was 
discovered in December&nbsp;2004 and is considered one of the best and largest 
remaining undeveloped resources in the U.K. North Sea. </font><font size="2" 
face="Arial" style="font-size:11.0pt;">The project is currently in the Front End
Engineering and Design (FEED) phase and has a design capacity of 100,000 barrels
of crude oil and 80 million cubic feet of natural gas per day.</font><font 
size="2" face="Arial" style="font-size:11.0pt;">The project is expected to be 
complementary to Suncor&#146;s existing UK portfolio. Subsequent to the 
successful close of this transaction, joint venture parties would be operator
Chevron North Sea Limited (40 per cent), Suncor (30 per cent), OMV (U.K.) 
Limited (20 per cent) and DONG E&amp;P (U.K.) Limited (10 per cent).</font></p>
