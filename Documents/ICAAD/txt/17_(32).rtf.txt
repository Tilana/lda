IN THE HIGH COURT OF FIJI
AT SUVA
CRIMINAL JURISDICTION

Criminal Case No: HAC 033 of 2005

STATE

v.

SENIVALATI RAMUWAI;
and RUPENI NAISORO

Hearing: 10th April  16th May 2007
Judgment: 16th May 2007

Counsel: Mr. D. Toganivalu & S. Vodokisolomoni for State
Ms S. Vaniqi for 1st Accused
Mr. S. Valenitabua for 2nd Accused

JUDGMENT

Two of the three assessors find the 1st Accused guilty of murder as charged. All assessors find the 2nd Accused guilty as charged.

I direct myself in accordance with my summing up and I agree with the majority opinion for the 1st Accused and the unanimous opinion for the 2nd Accused. I convict both Accused accordingly.

Nazhat Shameem
JUDGE

At Suva
16th May 2007
