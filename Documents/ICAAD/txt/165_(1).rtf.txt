IN THE HIGH COURT OF FIJI
AT SUVA

CRIMINAL APPEAL CASE NO.: HAA0121 OF 2004

BETWEEN:

AMENATAVE TUVUWAI LEWANIRABE
Appellant

- v -

STATE
Respondent

Counsel: Ms M. Mua  for the Appellant
Mr. P. Bulamainaivalu  for the State

Date of Hearing & Order: 16th November, 2004

ORDER

By consent and considerations of arguments, I order that

1.      
The appeal against conviction be granted without costs. File is returned to the Magistrates Court at Suva for re-trial.

2.      
The appellant is remanded on simple standard bail terms to appear in the Magistrates Court in Suva on Monday the 29th of November, 2004 at 9.30am for call-over.

Gerard Winter

At Suva
