PART III - NARRATIVE
State below in reasonable  detail the reasons why the Form 10-K and Form 10-KSB,
11-K, 20-F, 10-Q and Form 10-QSB,  N-SAR, or other transition  report or portion
thereof, could not be filed within the prescribed period.
The quarterly report of Dominovas Energy Corporation on Form 10-Q could not be
filed within the prescribed time period because the company was not able to
receive all financial reports and supporting documentation as necessary for
timely filing in a timely manner from third party entities that were in
possession of said documents to include; financial reports and supporting
documentation. The aforementioned are necessary for the timely filing of the
10-Q as well as an accurate 10-Q. Additionally, the receipt of same was
necessary for review by the company's registered public accountants prior to the
due date of the Report in order for submission of the 10-Q.
