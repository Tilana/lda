IN THE HIGH COURT OF FIJI
AT LAUTOKA
CRIMINAL JURISDICTION

CRIMINAL CASE NO. HAC 10 OF 2011

STATE

v

SAVENACA MILLER VUNISA

UNDERTAKING

I, SAVENACA MILLER VUNISA, in consideration of being conditionally released on Court probation pursuant to section 44 of the Sentencing and Penalties Decree 2009, HEREBY UNDERTAKE as follows:

1.      
I will appear in this Court on notice.

2.      
I will be of good behaviour for 2 years from today.

3.      
I will continue my education at T.P.A.F.

4.      
I will not enter any amusement centre, game centre or pool/billiards centre for 2 years from today.

5.      
I will continue to live with my parents, and will not live elsewhere without the leave of this Court.

DATED at Lautoka this 17th day of February 2011.

Savenaca Miller Vunisa

Paul K. Madigan
JUDGE
