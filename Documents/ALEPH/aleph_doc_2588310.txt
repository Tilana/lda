<h2>Notifiable trading</h2>
<p>On behalf of Statoil (OSE: STL, NYSE:STO), DNB has on 15th of September 2015 
purchased 781,215 shares for use in the group&acute;s Share saving plan. </p>
<p>The shares have been acquired at a price of NOK 119.16 per share. Before 
distribution to the employees, the Share saving plan has 10,437,546 shares. </p>
<p><em>This information is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to 
section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act. <br>
