<P STYLE="font: 10pt Arial,sans-serif; margin: 9.75pt 0">CALGARY, Sept. 11, 2015
/CNW/ - Vermilion Energy Inc. (&quot;Vermilion&quot;)
(TSX, NYSE: VET) is pleased to announce a cash dividend of $0.215 CDN per share 
payable on October 15, 2015 to all shareholders
