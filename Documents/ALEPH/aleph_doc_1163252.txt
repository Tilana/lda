Full Description of Material Change
Item 5.  
 
Vancouver, BC - Cypress Development Corp. (TSX-V: CYP) (OTC Pink: CYDVF) (Frankfurt: 
C1Z)  (the  “Company”)  announces  that  it  held  its  annual  general  meeting  on  May  29,  2014.   The 
Company reports that Donald C. Huston, James Pettit, Donald G. Myers and Amanda Chow were 
re-elected directors of the Company.  The shareholders of the Company also re-appointed Davidson 
& Company, Chartered Accountants, as auditors, approved the renewal of the Company's Incentive 
Stock Option Plan and approved to amend the exercise price of stock options previously granted to 
insiders.
