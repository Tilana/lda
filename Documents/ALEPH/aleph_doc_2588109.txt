<p style="text-align:center"><b>Ur-Energy Provides Pathfinder Mines Acquisition 
Update</b></p></div><div style="width:100%;margin-left:10pt;"><div 
style="margin:0;margin-top:16pt;"><p></p><p>LITTLETON, Colo., July 12, 2013 
/PRNewswire/ -- <b>Ur-Energy Inc. </b>(TSX:URE, NYSE MKT:URG)  (&#34;Ur-
Energy&#34; or the &#34;Company&#34;) is pleased to announce that the Company 
has received notice from the U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (&#34;NRC&#34;) 
of its approval of the transfer of the Pathfinder Mines Corporation 
(&#34;Pathfinder&#34;) Shirley Basin license.</p><p>(Logo: 
http://photos.prnewswire.com/prnh/20110913/LA67628LOGO)</p><p>The transfer 
approval is among the closing conditions of the Share Purchase Agreement with 
COGEMA Resources, Inc. (&#34;COGEMA&#34;), an affiliate of AREVA Mining, by 
which a U.S. subsidiary of Ur-Energy will acquire all the outstanding shares of
Pathfinder.  For additional details, please see the Company&#39;s July 24, 2012 
news release.  The NRC also approved the transfer to COGEMA of the NRC license 
for the Pathfinder Lucky Mc tailing site.  Ur-Energy will assume no obligations 
for the tailing site, which is fully reclaimed and in the process of being 
transferred to the U.S. Department of Energy. The Company and COGEMA continue to
advance the other remaining governmental approvals and to satisfy additional 
customary closing conditions toward completing the acquisition anticipated 
during the third quarter this year.</p><p>Ur-Energy CEO Wayne Heili said, 
&#34;The completion of the Pathfinder transaction remains an integral element of
Ur-Energy&#39;s U.S. based in-situ uranium recovery strategy.  We look forward 
to closing the acquisition in the near future.&#34;   </p><p><b><u>About Ur-
Energy
<br /></u></b>Ur-Energy is a junior uranium mining company currently 
constructing its first in-situ recovery (ISR) uranium mine in south- central 
Wyoming at its fully licensed and permitted Lost Creek project.  The Lost Creek 
processing facility will have two million pounds per year capacity and is 
anticipated to be in production in the second half of 2013. Ur-Energy engages in
the identification, acquisition, exploration and development of uranium projects
in the United States and Canada.  Shares of Ur-Energy trade on the Toronto Stock
Exchange under the symbol &#34;URE&#34; and on the NYSE MKT under the symbol 
&#34;URG&#34;. Ur-Energy&#39;s corporate office is located in Littleton, 
Colorado; its registered office is in Ottawa, Ontario.  Ur-Energy&#39;s website 
is www.ur-energy.com.</p><p><b>FOR FURTHER INFORMATION, PLEASE 
CONTACT</b></p><div>
