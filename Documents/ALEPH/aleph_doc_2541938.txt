<div style="MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 
0pt" align="center"><font style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; 
FONT-WEIGHT: bold; DISPLAY: inline">ENTR&#201;E GOLD APPOINTS ANNA STYLIANIDES 
TO BOARD OF DIRECTORS</font></div>
<div style="MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 
0pt" align="center">&#160;</div>
<div style="MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 
0pt" align="justify"><font style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman;
DISPLAY: inline"><font style="FONT-WEIGHT: bold; DISPLAY: inline">Vancouver, 
B.C., July 14, 2015 </font>&#8211; Entr&#233;e Gold Inc. (TSX:ETG; NYSE MKT:EGI;
Frankfurt:EKA - "Entr&#233;e" or the "Company") has appointed Anna Stylianides 
to its Board of Directors.</font></div>
<div style="MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 
0pt" align="justify">&#160;</div>
<div style="MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 
0pt" align="justify"><font style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman;
DISPLAY: inline">Ms. Stylianides has over 20 years of experience in global 
capital markets and has spent much of her career in investment banking, private 
equity, and corporate management and restructuring.&#160;&#160;She began her 
career in corporate law by joining the firm of Webber Wentzel Attorneys in 1990 
after graduating from the University of the Witwatersrand in Johannesburg, South
Africa.&#160;&#160;In 1992, she joined Investec Merchant Bank Limited where she 
specialized in risk management and gained extensive experience in the areas of 
corporate finance, structured finance, mergers and acquisitions, structuring, 
specialized finance and other banking and financial services 
transactions.&#160;&#160;She was also involved in designing and structuring of 
financial products for financial institutions and corporations.</font></div>
<div style="MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 
0pt" align="justify">&#160;</div>
<div style="MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 
0pt" align="justify"><font style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman;
DISPLAY: inline">Ms. Styliandes is currently the Co-Chairman and CEO of Eco Oro 
Minerals Corp. (formerly Greystar Resources) and a director of Capfin Partners, 
LLC, Altius Minerals Corporation and the Fraser Institute.</font></div>
<div style="MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 
0pt" align="justify">&#160;</div>
<div style="MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 
0pt" align="justify"><font style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman;
DISPLAY: inline">Ms. Stylianides has also been a director of and engaged in the 
financial restructuring of several NASDAQ publicly traded companies, gaining 
extensive knowledge of Canadian and SEC securities regulations. She has had 
numerous board appointments, including Callinan Royalties Corporation and 
Alphamin Resources Corporation.&#160;&#160;Additionally, Ms. Stylianides was 
previously a director and CEO of Surgical Spaces Inc. Group of Companies and 
oversaw its national expansion strategy to become one of Canada&#8217;s private 
healthcare consolidators prior to its sale to Centric Health Corporation in 
2011.</font></div>
<div style="MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 
0pt" align="justify">&#160;</div>
<div style="MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 
0pt" align="justify"><font style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman;
DISPLAY: inline">Lord Howard of Lympne, Chairman of Entr&#233;e, commented, 
&#8220;Anna is a welcome addition to our Board of Directors, as someone who will
bring a depth of experience that can only enhance and strengthen our entire 
team.&#160;&#160;I am very much looking forward to working with Anna as we 
continue to move our Company forward.&#8221;</font></div>
<div style="MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 
0pt" align="justify">&#160;</div>
<div style="MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT:
0pt" align="left"><font style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; 
FONT-WEIGHT: bold; DISPLAY: inline">ABOUT ENTR&#201;E GOLD INC.</font></div>
<div style="MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 
0pt" align="left">&#160;</div>
<div style="MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 
0pt" align="justify"><font style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman;
DISPLAY: inline">Entr&#233;e Gold Inc. is a Canadian mineral exploration company
balancing opportunity and risk with key assets in Nevada and 
Mongolia.&#160;&#160;Entr&#233;e has been advancing its Ann Mason Project in one
of the world&#8217;s most favourable mining jurisdictions, Nevada and recently 
completed a pre-feasibility level drill program.&#160;&#160;The Ann Mason 
Project hosts the Ann Mason copper-molybdenum deposit as well as the Blue Hill 
copper deposit within the rejuvenated Yerington copper camp.&#160;</font></div>
<div style="MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 
0pt" align="justify">&#160;</div>
<div style="MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 
0pt" align="justify"><font style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman;
DISPLAY: inline">Additionally, as a joint venture partner with a carried 
interest on a portion of the Oyu Tolgoi mining project in Mongolia, Entr&#233;e 
has a unique opportunity to participate in one of the world&#8217;s largest 
copper-gold projects managed by one of the premier mining companies &#8211; Rio 
Tinto.&#160;&#160;Oyu Tolgoi, with its series of deposits containing copper, 
gold and molybdenum, has been under exploration and development since the late 
1990s.</font></div>
<div style="MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 
0pt" align="justify">&#160;</div>
<div style="MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 
0pt" align="justify"><font style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman;
DISPLAY: inline">Sandstorm Gold, Rio Tinto and Turquoise Hill Resources are 
major shareholders of Entr&#233;e, holding approximately 12%, 11% and 9% of 
issued and outstanding shares, respectively.</font></div>
