One Count

 [Representative Count]
Statement of Offence (a)

RAPE: Contrary to section 207 (1) and (2) (a) of the Crimes Decree 44 of 2009.

Particulars of Offence

MARISILINO TABAKANACA between the 1st day of January 2011 to the 31st day of July 2011, at Nasinu, in the Central Division, had carnal knowledge of M.L., without her consent.

3.
