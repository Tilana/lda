Sentence : 25th May, 2012

SENTENCE

1.      
I have taken into account the accused's plea in mitigation, as submitted by defence counsel.

2.      
I have taken into account the prosecution's submission.

3.      
The accused is a 1st offender. He has been remanded in custody for 1 year 2 months. In my view, he has already served his punishment, for count no. 1.

4.      
There is no further need for punishment.

5.      
Accused is discharged on count no. 1, given that he has already served his sentence, by been remanded in custody for 1 year 2 months.

6.      
He is released forthwith.

Salesi Temo
JUDGE
