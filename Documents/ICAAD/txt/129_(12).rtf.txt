IN THE HIGH COURT OF FIJI
AT LABASA
CRIMINAL JURISDICTION

CRIMINAL CASE NO: HAC 043 OF 2009

STATE

V

BENI BALEIMUALEVU SALAGI

ORDER

I hereby order pursuant to section 105 of the Criminal Procedure Decree 2009 that the accused Beni Baleimualevu Salagi be held confined in the St. Giles Psychiatric Hospital, Suva in the custody of the Medical Superintendent until order by His Excellency the President of Fiji.

Daniel Goundar
JUDGE

At Labasa
15th April 2010
