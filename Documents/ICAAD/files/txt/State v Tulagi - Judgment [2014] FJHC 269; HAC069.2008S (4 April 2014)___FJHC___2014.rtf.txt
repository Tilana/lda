JUDGMENT

1.      
Two of the assessors have found the accused not guilty as charged on all counts. 

2.      
One assessor found the accused not guilty as charged on counts nos. 1 and 4, but guilty on counts nos. 2 and 3.

3.      
The final judgment on the guilt or otherwise of the accused, is the prerogative of the trial judge.

4.      
In this case, I disagree with the not guilty opinions of all the assessors.

5.      
I find the accused guilty as charged on all counts and convict him accordingly.

6.      
I will give my written reasons when I deliver my sentence.

7.      
Assessors thanked and released.

Salesi Temo
