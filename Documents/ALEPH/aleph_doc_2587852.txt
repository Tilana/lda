<P STYLE="font: bold 24pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin-right: 0; margin-
left: 0">ARC Resources Ltd. confirms September
15, 2015 dividend amount</P>
<P STYLE="font: 12pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin-right: 0; margin-left:
0">CALGARY, Aug. 17, 2015 /CNW/ - <B>(ARX - TSX)</B>
ARC Resources Ltd. (&quot;ARC&quot;) confirms that an eligible dividend of $0.10
per share will be paid on September 15, 2015 to
shareholders of record on August 31, 2015.&nbsp; The ex-dividend date is August 
27, 2015.&nbsp; As at August 17, 2015 the trailing
twelve-month payments to investors, including the August 17, 2015 payment, total
$1.20 per share.&nbsp;</P>
<P STYLE="font: 12pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin-right: 0; margin-left:
0">ARC offers a Dividend Reinvestment Plan (&quot;DRIP&quot;)
and Stock Dividend Program (&quot;SDP&quot;) as optional plans for its 
shareholders. The&nbsp;DRIP provides the option for Canadian
holders of ARC common shares to have their cash dividends reinvested into 
additional common shares of ARC. The SDP provides the
option for shareholders to receive dividends in the form of common shares of ARC
in lieu of receiving a cash dividend on the dividend
payment date. Common shares issued under the DRIP and SDP are issued at a three 
per cent discount to the prevailing market price
with no broker fees or commissions.&nbsp;Participation in the DRIP and/or SDP is
optional; shareholders who do not wish to participate
in either plan will continue to receive cash dividends.&nbsp; Additional 
information on the DRIP and SDP including the Dividend
Reinvestment/Stock Dividend Enrollment Form can be found on the company's 
website at www.arcresources.com or by contacting your
financial institution or investment advisor. &nbsp;The availability of the SDP 
and DRIP and the respective terms and conditions
are subject to the discretion of ARC's management and the Board of 
Directors.</P>
<P STYLE="font: 12pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin-right: 0; margin-left:
0">ARC is one of Canada's largest conventional
oil and gas companies with an enterprise value of approximately $7.3 
billion.&nbsp; ARC's common shares trade on the TSX under
the symbol ARX.</P>
