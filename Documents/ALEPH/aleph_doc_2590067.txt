<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" 
style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Vale prices US$1.0 billion notes due 
2026</font></b></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" 
style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" 
style="font-size:10.0pt;">Rio de Janeiro, August&nbsp;3, 2016 &#151; Vale S.A. 
(Vale) hereby announces the pricing of the offering by its wholly owned 
subsidiary Vale Overseas Limited (Vale Overseas) of US$1,000,000,000&#160;
6.250% Guaranteed Notes due 2026.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" 
style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" 
style="font-size:10.0pt;">The notes will bear a coupon of 6.250% per year, 
payable semi-annually, and were sold at a price of 100.000% of the principal 
amount.&#160; These notes will mature on August&nbsp;10, 2026 and were priced 
with a spread of 470.3 basis points over U.S. Treasuries, resulting in a yield 
to maturity of 6.250%.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" 
style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" 
style="font-size:10.0pt;">The notes are rated BBB- by Standard&nbsp;&amp; 
Poor&#146;s Rating Services, Ba3 by Moody&#146;s Investor Services, BBB low by 
Dominion Bond Rating Service and BBB by Fitch Ratings.&#160; The notes will be 
unsecured obligations of Vale Overseas and will be fully and unconditionally 
guaranteed by Vale.&#160; The guaranty will rank equally in right of payment 
with all of Vale&#146;s other unsecured and unsubordinated debt 
obligations.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" 
style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" 
style="font-size:10.0pt;">Vale intends to use the net proceeds from the offering
to pay part of the redemption price of the 6.250% notes due 2017, issued by Vale
Overseas and guaranteed by Vale S.A., which mature in 
January&nbsp;2017.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" 
style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" 
style="font-size:10.0pt;">Banco Bradesco BBI S.A., BB Securities Ltd., BNP 
Paribas Securities Corp., Citigroup Global Markets Inc., and Morgan 
Stanley&nbsp;&amp; Co. LLC acted as joint lead managers and joint bookrunners 
and CIBC World Markets Corp., Credit Agricole Securities (USA) Inc., Mizuho 
Securities USA Inc., MUFG Securities Americas Inc., Natixis Securities Americas 
LLC, SG Americas Securities, LLC and SMBC Nikko Securities America,&nbsp;Inc. 
acted as co-managers.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" 
style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" 
style="font-size:10.0pt;">Vale and Vale Overseas have filed a registration 
statement, including a prospectus, with the U.S. Securities and Exchange 
Commission (SEC) for the offering of the notes.&#160; Before you invest, you 
should read the prospectus in that registration statement and other documents 
Vale and Vale Overseas have filed with the SEC for more complete information 
about the companies and the offering.&#160; When available, you may access these
documents for free by visiting EDGAR on the SEC Web site at www.sec.gov. 
Alternatively, the underwriters will arrange to send you the prospectus if you 
request it by calling BNP Paribas Securities Corp. at +1 (800) 854-5674, 
Citigroup Global Markets Inc. at +1 (800) 831-9146 or Morgan Stanley&nbsp;&amp; 
Co. LLC at 1 (866) 718-1649&#160; (each toll-free in the United States) or, if 
calling from outside the U.S., by collect calling BB Securities Ltd. at +44 (20)
7367 5800 att. Operations Department&#160; or Banco Bradesco BBI S.A. at +1 
(212) 888-9145.</font></p>
