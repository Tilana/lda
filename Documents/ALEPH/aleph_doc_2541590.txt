EXHIBITS
Exhibit 99
----------
Press release issued September 27, 2005 in which the Registrant announced it has
arranged a private placement with its largest shareholder, Pan Atlantic Bank and
Trust Limited, consisting of 1.0 million common shares at $5.00 per share for
proceeds of $5,000,000. The price reflects a 4.8% discount from the Registrant's
stock closing price on September 26, 2005. This financing will be used to fund
an expanded exploration program at two of its Nevada exploration projects and
general working capital requirements.
