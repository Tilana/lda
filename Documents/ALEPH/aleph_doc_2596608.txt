<P style="margin:0px; font-family:Calibri,Times New Roman; font-size:12pt" 
align=justify>Buenos Aires, July 28, 2016 <font style='font-family:Arial Unicode
MS,Times New Roman'>&#8211;</font> Petrobras Argentina S.A. (Buenos Aires: PESA 
<font style='font-family:Arial Unicode MS,Times New Roman'>&#8211;</font> NYSE:
PZE) announces that on July 27, 2016 Pampa Energ&#237;a S.A. acquired the shares
representing 100% of the capital stock and voting rights of Petrobras 
Participaciones S.L., controlling company of PESA as holder of 67.1933% of the 
capital stock and voting rights of the Company (the &quot;Transaction&quot;). As
a result of the Transaction, the change of indirect controlling shareholder of 
the Company was completed, with Pampa Energ&#237;a S.A. being the new indirect 
controlling shareholder of PESA.</P>
<P style="margin-top:8px; margin-bottom:0px" align=justify><BR>
<BR></P>
<P style="margin-top:8px; margin-bottom:0px; font-family:Calibri,Times New 
Roman; font-size:12pt; page-break-before:always" align=justify>As a result of 
the closing of the Transaction, all regular directors of the Company, to wit: 
&nbsp;Andre Lima Cordeiro, Jorge Jose Nahas Neto, Guilherme Pontes Galv&#227;o 
Fran&#231;a, Carlos Alberto Pereira de Olivera, Cedric Bridger, Roberto Luis 
Monti, Roberto Alejandro Fortunati, Marcos Benicio Pompa Antunes and Maelcio 
Mauricio Soares, as well as all alternate directors, to wit: Gustavo Tardin 
Barbosa, Mauro Roberto Da Costa Mendes, Alejandro Polleto, Claudio Celestino 
Linassi Mastella, Gustavo Adolfo Amaral, Jose Jorge De Moraes Junior, 
H&#233;ctor Daniel Casal and Santiago Luis Montezanti submitted their 
resignation on the date hereof.</P>
<P style="margin-top:8px; margin-bottom:0px; font-family:Calibri,Times New 
Roman; font-size:12pt" align=justify>According to the provisions of Section 258 
of the General Companies Law, &nbsp;the Company<font style='font-family:Arial 
Unicode MS,Times New Roman'>&#8217;</font>s Statutory Syndic Committee appointed
on the date hereof the following regular and alternate directors: Marcos Marcelo
Mindlin, Gustavo Mariani, Dami&#225;n Miguel Mindlin, Ricardo Alejandro Torres, 
Diego Mart&#237;n Salaverri, Horacio Turri, Marcelo Blanco, H&#233;ctor 
Moch&#243;n and Miguel Bein as regular directors, and Ariel Schapira, Gabriel 
Cohen, Mariano Batistella, Nicol&#225;s Mindlin, Tom&#225;s Mindlin, Victoria 
Hitce, Carlos Correa Urquiza, Enrique Luj&#225;n Benitez and Jos&#233; Ramos as 
alternate directors.</P>
<P style="margin-top:8px; margin-bottom:0px; font-family:Calibri,Times New 
Roman; font-size:12pt" align=justify>Regular Directors Marcelo Blanco, 
H&#233;ctor Mochon and Miguel Bein and alternate directors Carlos Correa 
Urquiza, Enrique Luj&#225;n Benitez and Jos&#233; Ramos qualify as independent 
directors under Section 11, Chapter III, Title II of the Argentine Securities 
Commission Regulations and the Securities and Exchange Commission and the New 
York Stock Exchange Rules and Regulations. The remaining regular and alternate 
directors are non-independent under the above mentioned rules and 
regulations.</P>
<P style="margin-top:8px; margin-bottom:0px; font-family:Calibri,Times New 
Roman; font-size:12pt" align=justify>Marcos Marcelo Mindlin was appointed 
Chairman of the Company<font style='font-family:Arial Unicode MS,Times New 
Roman'>&#8217;</font>s Board of Directors and Gustavo Mariani Vice Chairman.</P>
<P style="margin-top:8px; margin-bottom:0px; font-family:Calibri,Times New 
Roman; font-size:12pt" align=justify>In addition, and as a result of the 
resignation of all directors who were members of the Company<font style='font-
family:Arial Unicode MS,Times New Roman'>&#8217;</font>s Audit Committee, at the
meeting held on the date hereof the Board of Directors resolved to appoint 
regular directors Marcelo Blanco, H&#233;ctor Moch&#243;n and Miguel Bein as 
regular members of the Audit Committee and alternate directors Carlos Correa 
Urquiza, Enrique Luj&#225;n Benitez and Jos&#233; Ramos as alternate members of 
the Audit Committee, all of them being independent directors as stated 
above.</P>
