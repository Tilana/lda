<P STYLE="font-size: 10pt; margin: 0"><B>Statoil ASA: Publication of prospectus 
and commencement of subscription period for the
Dividend Issue for the first quarter 2016 under the Scrip Dividend Programme - 
FOR SHAREHOLDERS IN NORWAY</B></P>
<P STYLE="font-size: 10pt; margin: 0">&nbsp;</P>
<P STYLE="font-size: 10pt; margin-right: 0; margin-left: 0"><B>Reference is made
to the previous announcements by Statoil ASA (OSE:STL,
NYSE:STO,&nbsp;the &quot;Company&quot;) regarding the resolution made by the 
annual general meeting on 11 May 2016 to approve the
two year scrip dividend programme commencing from the fourth quarter 2015 (the 
&quot;Scrip Dividend Programme&quot;) and to distribute
a dividend of USD 0.2201 (NOK 1.8255, as announced 17 August 2016) per share for
the first quarter of 2016 that shareholders can
choose to receive either in new shares of the Company (&quot;Dividend 
Shares&quot;) or in cash.</B></P>
<P STYLE="font-size: 10pt; margin-right: 0; margin-left: 0">In connection with 
the Scrip Dividend Programme for the first quarter
2016 (the &quot;Dividend Issue&quot;), the Company will issue up to 160,000,000 
Dividend Shares, each with a nominal value of NOK
2.50 per share, for subscription by Existing Shareholders (as described 
below).</P>
<P STYLE="font-size: 10pt; margin-right: 0; margin-left: 0"><B>Approved 
Prospectus:</B><BR>
The Norwegian Financial Supervisory Authority (Finanstilsynet) approved on 27 
August 2016 the prospectus for the offering of the
Dividend Shares to Existing Shareholders in Norway.</P>
<P STYLE="font-size: 10pt; margin-right: 0; margin-left: 0">The Prospectus is 
available at www.statoil.com/scrip, where hard copies
may also be ordered.</P>
<P STYLE="font-size: 10pt; margin-right: 0; margin-left: 0"><B>Subscription 
period:</B><BR>
The subscription period for the Dividend Issue will commence at 09:00 a.m. CEST
on 29 August 2016 and expire at 23:59 p.m. CEST
on 9 September 2016 (the &quot;Subscription Period&quot;).</P>
<P STYLE="font-size: 10pt; margin-right: 0; margin-left: 0">Existing 
Shareholders who have not subscribed for Dividend Shares at
the time of expiry of the Subscription Period will receive their dividend in 
cash without any action on their part on or about
23 September 2016.</P>
<P STYLE="font-size: 10pt; margin-right: 0; margin-left: 0"><B>Subscription 
price:</B><BR>
The subscription price will be set as the volume-weighted average share price 
over the last two trading days on Oslo B&oslash;rs
(the &quot;Oslo Stock Exchange&quot;) of the Subscription Period for the 
Dividend Issue, with a deduction for a discount of 5%.
The subscription price is expected to be announced on or about 12 September 
2016.</P>
<P STYLE="font-size: 10pt; margin-right: 0; margin-left: 0"><B>Subscription 
procedures:</B><BR>
Subscriptions for Dividend Shares can be made electronically via VPS' online 
subscription system. A link to the subscription system
can be found on www.statoil.com/scrip. Existing Shareholders who are considered 
eligible to subscribe for Dividend Shares have
or will receive a letter from the Company with personal log in details to the 
VPS subscription system.</P>
<P STYLE="font-size: 10pt; margin-right: 0; margin-left: 0"><B>Eligibility to 
participate in the Dividend Issue:</B><BR>
Shareholders of the Company holding shares as of 9 August 2016 and who were 
registered with the Company's shareholder register
in the Norwegian central security depository (VPS) as of close of business 11 
August 2016 (the record date) (&quot;Existing Shareholders&quot;)
are entitled to elect to either subscribe for Dividend Shares during the 
Subscription Period or receive their dividend in cash.</P>
<P STYLE="font-size: 10pt; margin-right: 0; margin-left: 0">The subscription for
Dividend Shares by persons located in, or resident
of countries other than Norway, may be affected by the laws of the relevant 
jurisdiction. The Dividend Shares may not be subscribed
for by Existing Shareholders located in or resident of, jurisdictions in which 
the subscription for Dividend Shares would be unlawful.
Existing Shareholders located in, or resident of, countries other than Norway, 
should inform themselves as to whether restrictions
apply to the Dividend Issues and consult their professional advisors if they are
in any doubt about any of the contents or application
of these restrictions.</P>
<P STYLE="font-size: 10pt; margin-right: 0; margin-left: 
0"><B>Settlement:</B><BR>
The issue of the Dividend Shares is expected to take place on or about 22 
September 2016, and the delivery of the Dividend Shares
on Oslo B&oslash;rs is expected to take place on or about 23 September 2016. 
Delivery of the ADRs representing Dividend Shares
is expected to take place on or about 26 September 2016. Trading in the Dividend
Shares on Oslo B&oslash;rs and in the ADRs representing
Dividend Shares on NYSE is expected to commence on or about 26 September 
2016.</P>
<P STYLE="font-size: 10pt; margin-right: 0; margin-left: 0">Cash payment of the 
dividend to holders of ordinary shares on Oslo
B&oslash;rs is expected to be on or about 23 September 2016. Cash payment of the
dividend to holders of ADRs is expected to be
on or about 26 September 2016.</P>
<P STYLE="font-size: 10pt; margin-right: 0; margin-left: 0"><B>Interests held 
through financial intermediaries:</B><BR>
Shareholders, who are eligible for participation in the Dividend Issue and who 
hold their Shares through a financial intermediary
and wish to subscribe for Dividend Shares in the Dividend Issue, should instruct
their financial intermediary to subscribe for
Dividend Shares in accordance with the applicable instructions received from 
such financial intermediary. The financial intermediary
will be responsible for collecting exercise instructions from the shareholders 
and for informing the Company of their subscription
instructions.</P>
<P STYLE="font-size: 10pt; margin-right: 0; margin-left: 0">DNB Bank ASA, 
Registrars Department (tel.: +47 23 26 80 20) is acting
as Receiving Agent for the Dividend Issue.</P>
