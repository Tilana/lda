RESULTS OF ANNUAL GENERAL MEETING
The Company held an Annual General Meeting today, 2 September 2016 at 10.00am (WST). 
 
The resolutions voted were in accordance with the Notice of Annual General Meeting previously advised 
to the Australian Securities Exchange. 
 
Further information, required by Section 251AA(2) of the Corporations Act 2001 (Cth), is attached.
