The Shareholders of Golden Chalice Resources Inc.

Golden  Chalice  Resources  Inc.  (the  “Company”)  will  be  held  in  the  boardroom  of  Suite  1290,  625 
Howe Street, Vancouver, British Columbia, V6C 2T6, on Wednesday, October 6, 2010, at 1:30 p.m., 
for the following purposes: 
1.
To receive and consider the comparative financial statements of the Company for the financial 
year ended April 30, 2010, together with the report of the auditor thereon; 
To  elect  directors  for  the  ensuing  year  or  until  their  successors  have  been  duly  elected  or 
appointed;

of the Company’s shares on a 9:1 basis;

the Company’s name; and
