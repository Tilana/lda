<div style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial; TEXT-ALIGN: center; MARGIN-TOP:
12pt">Teck Announces Acquisition of Securities of Jet Gold</div>
<div style="MARGIN-TOP: 12pt"><br />
</div>
<div style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN-TOP: 
12pt"><font style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial; FONT-WEIGHT: 
bold">Vancouver, B.C.</font> &#8211; Teck Resources Limited (TSX: TCK.A and 
TCK.B, NYSE: TCK) (&#8220;Teck&#8221;) announced today that it acquired 
8,333,333<font style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial; FONT-WEIGHT: 
bold">&#160;</font>common shares, and a $389,117<font style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; 
FONT-FAMILY: Arial; FONT-WEIGHT: bold">&#160;</font>principal amount convertible
debenture of Jet Gold Corp. (&#8220;Jet Gold&#8221;). Jet Gold issued the 
securities in satisfaction of $889,117 of indebtedness owing to a Teck 
subsidiary. Jet Gold had assumed the indebtedness in connection with its 
indirect acquisition of control over a 30% interest in Haib Minerals (PTY) Ltd.,
in which Teck indirectly holds a 70% interest. The common shares were issued for
an aggregate price of $500,000, and the convertible debenture was issued for a 
price of $389,117. No cash consideration was paid by Teck and the purchase price
was fully satisfied by set-off against the assumed indebtedness.</div>
<div style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN-TOP: 
12pt">As a result of the transaction, Teck holds 8,333,333 Jet Gold common 
shares, approximately 11.6<font style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial; 
FONT-WEIGHT: bold">%</font> of Jet Gold&#8217;s outstanding common shares, and a
$389,117<font style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial; FONT-WEIGHT: 
bold">&#160;</font>principal amount convertible debenture. The debenture matures
in 48 months and is convertible at Teck&#8217;s election, in whole or in part, 
into Jet Gold common shares at a price of $0.07 per share. If Teck were<font 
style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial"> to convert the entire principal 
amount of the debenture, Teck would hold approximately </font>17.9<font 
style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">% of Jet Gold&#8217;s outstanding 
common shares, calculated on a partially diluted basis assuming the conversion 
of the debenture only.</font></div>
<div style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN-TOP: 
12pt">Teck may determine to increase or decrease its holdings in Jet Gold 
depending on market conditions and any other relevant factors. This release is 
required to be issued under the early warning requirements of applicable 
securities laws. A copy of the early warning report may be obtained from the 
contacts listed below.</div>
