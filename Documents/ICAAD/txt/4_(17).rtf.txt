The Hon. Justice Gordon Ward, Justice of Appeal

Hearing: Wednesday, 23 February 2000

Counsel: Mr V Kapadia for the Appellant
Mr R Prakash for the Respondents

Date of Judgment: Thursday, 24 February 2000

DECISION OF THE COURT

The appellant seeks leave under S.122(2)(a) of the Constitution to appeal to the Supreme Court against the judgment of this Court dated 26 November 1999. Leaving aside the submission by respondents counsel that no appeal lies in this case, we are satisfied that the questions sought to be raised are not of significant public importance. They are of concern only to the parties themselves.

Leave to appeal is refused.
