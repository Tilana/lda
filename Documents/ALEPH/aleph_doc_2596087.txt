PJSC LukOil

PJSC "LUKOIL" announces that a copy of the Annual Report of PJSC "LUKOIL" for 2015
prepared in accordance with the requirements of the Russian legislation and tentatively
approved by the Board of Directors of PJSC "LUKOIL" on 13 May 2016, for submission to the

and will shortly be available for inspection at http://www.morningstar.co.uk/uk/NSM .
