IN THE HIGH COURT OF FIJI
AT LAUTOKA
CRIMINAL JURISDICTION

CRIMINAL CASE NO. HAC0023 OF 2004L

THE STATE

v

PENIASI TUILASELASE

Mr. N. Nand for the State
Accused in Person

Date of Hearing: 16 August 2005
Date of Judgment: 16 August 2005

JUDGMENT

Peniasi Tuilaselase, the assessors have unanimously found you not guilty.

Directing myself in accordance with my summing up, I do not disagree with the opinions they have expressed, you are acquitted and I direct that you be set at liberty.

JOHN CONNORS
JUDGE

At Lautoka
16 August 2005
