Press Release 
 
Vale pays interest on infrastructure debentures
 
Rio de Janeiro, August 12th, 2016 – Vale S.A. (Vale) informs that it will pay the interest on its
shareholders debentures (debentures) on August 15th, 2016, through the Banco Bradesco S.A.
The unit prices are: R$ 66.00127821 for each of the 800,000 debentures of the 1st Series and R$
65.72091469 for each of the 550,000 debentures of the 2nd Series. The total amount to be paid
on August 15th, 2016 is R$ 88,947,525.65.
