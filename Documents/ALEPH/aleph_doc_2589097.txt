<P style="margin:0px; padding-right:2.067px; text-indent:-2px; font-family:Book

Material Change</B></P>
<P style="margin:0px; clear:left" align=justify><BR></P>
<P style="margin:0px; padding-left:96px; padding-right:2.067px; font-family:Book
Antiqua,Times New Roman; font-size:11pt" align=justify>On July 28, 2016, Fortuna
completed its previously announced acquisition of all of the issued and 
outstanding shares of Goldrock by way of plan of arrangement. Goldrock is now a 
wholly-owned subsidiary of Fortuna.</P>
<P style="margin:0px" align=justify><BR></P>
<P style="margin:0px; padding-left:96px; padding-right:2.067px; font-family:Book
Antiqua,Times New Roman; font-size:11pt" align=justify>Pursuant to the 
Arrangement, Goldrock shareholders received 0.1331 of a common share of Fortuna 
(each whole share, a &#147;<B>Fortuna Share</B>&#148;) for each Goldrock Share 
held. Additionally, outstanding warrants to purchase Goldrock Shares are now 
exercisable for Fortuna Shares based on the same exchange ratio.</P>
<P style="margin:0px" align=justify><BR></P>
<P style="margin:0px; padding-left:96px; padding-right:2.067px; font-family:Book
Antiqua,Times New Roman; font-size:11pt" align=justify>The Arrangement was 
approved by the Supreme Court of British Columbia in its final order dated July 
26, 2016. The Arrangement remains subject to final approval by the Toronto Stock
Exchange, the New York Stock Exchange and the TSX Venture Exchange (the 
&#147;<B>TSX-V</B>&#148;).</P>
<P style="margin:0px" align=justify><BR></P>
<P style="margin:0px; padding-left:96px; padding-right:2.067px; font-family:Book
Antiqua,Times New Roman; font-size:11pt" align=justify>The delisting of the 
Goldrock Shares from the TSX-V is expected to occur at the close of business on 
or about July 28, 2016 and applications will be made for Goldrock to cease being
a reporting issuer in the applicable jurisdictions.</P>
<P style="margin:0px" align=justify><BR></P>
<P style="margin:0px; padding-left:96px; padding-right:2.067px; font-family:Book
Antiqua,Times New Roman; font-size:11pt" align=justify>Full details of the 
Arrangement are set out in Goldrock&#146;s management information circular dated
June 17, 2016, which is filed under Goldrock&#146;s SEDAR profile at <FONT 
style="color:#0000FF"><U>www.sedar.com</U></FONT>.</P>
