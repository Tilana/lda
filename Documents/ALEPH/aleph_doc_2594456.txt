CONSENT OF INDEPENDENT PETROLEUM ENGINEERS
The undersigned hereby consents to the references to our firm in the form and context in which
they appear in this Registration Statement on Form S-3 of Continental Resources, Inc. and to the
reference of our report, dated January 24, 2016, setting forth estimates of Continental Resources,
Inc.’s proved crude oil and natural gas reserves and related future net revenues in such

Report on Form 10-K and as an exhibit to the Annual Report on Form 10-K relating to our report
setting forth the estimates of Continental Resources, Inc.’s proved crude oil and natural gas
reserves and related future net revenues as of December 31, 2015.
