<P align=center><B>ROYAL STANDARD SECURES USD$2,000,000 LOAN EXTENSION FROM
<BR>WATERTON GLOBAL VALUE </B></P>
<P align=justify>Toronto, Ontario &#150; June 28, 2012 &#150; Royal Standard 
Minerals Inc.
(OTCBB: RYSMF) (the &#147;Company&#148; or &#147;Royal Standard&#148;) is 
pleased to announce that
it has secured a loan extension from Waterton Global Value, L.P. 
(&#147;Waterton&#148;).
The loan extension is intended to enable Royal Standard to meet its company
goals, despite setbacks in their intended timeline due to legacy issues. The
Company has been in the final stages of commissioning the mill at Goldwedge, the
Company&#146;s flagship project, and the Company continues to explore its 
additional
properties including the Pinon property. </P>
<P align=justify>Philip Gross, Interim President and Chief Executive Officer of
Royal Standard Minerals, stated, &#147;This loan extension provides the support 
that
we need as we continue to determine the best means of delivering value.&#148; 
Cheryl
Brandon, Portfolio Manager at Waterton, stated, &#147;We continue to see 
potential in
Royal Standard and are pleased to continue to support the Company as it works
toward reaching its goals.&#148; </P>
<P align=justify>As consideration for the USD$2,000,000 loan extension, the
Company provided Waterton with a 2% structuring fee relating to the loan
extension. No additional consideration (whether in royalty form or otherwise)
was provided to Waterton. </P>
<P align=justify><B>About Royal Standard Minerals Inc. </B></P>
<P align=justify>Royal Standard Minerals is a junior mining and exploration
company with multiple high quality gold projects in Nevada, USA. The 
Company&#146;s
flagship, Goldwedge Mine, is located southeast of the Round Mountain gold mine
in central Nevada. Royal Standard explores and develops gold and polymetallic
properties. The Company&#146;s current portfolio of gold exploration projects
includes Fondaway Canyon, Pinon, and Dixie Comstock. The Company intends to
aggressively explore and develop its Nevada gold projects and acquire additional
prospective gold properties, to create a development pipeline that can fuel
further growth. </P>
