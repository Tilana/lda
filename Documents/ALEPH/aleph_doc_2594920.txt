<div style="MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 
0pt" align="left"><font style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; 
FONT-WEIGHT: bold; DISPLAY: inline">Item 4.02(a). Non-Reliance on Previously 
Issued Financial Statements or a Related Audit Report or Completed Interim 
Review.</font></div>
<div style="DISPLAY: block; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt"><br>
</div>
<div style="MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 
0pt" align="left"><font style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; 
FONT-WEIGHT: bold; FONT-STYLE: italic; DISPLAY: inline">Overview</font></div>
<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-RIGHT:
0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt">&#160;</div>
<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-RIGHT:
0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt"><font style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New 
Roman; DISPLAY: inline">On August 2, 2016, the audit committee of the board of 
directors (the &#8220;Audit Committee&#8221;) and management of Lode-Star Mining
Inc.(the &#8220;Company&#8221;), after discussions with the Company&#8217;s 
independent registered public accounting firm, concluded that the financial 
statements for the year ended December 31, 2015 and the quarterly period ended 
March 31, 2016 (the &#8220;Financial Periods&#8221;) which are included in the 
Company&#8217;s Reports on Forms 10-K and 10-Q respectively for such periods, 
should no longer be relied upon and need to be restated, due to a non-cash error
in the application of accruals with respect to a single, long-term
contract.</font></div>
<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify; DISPLAY: block; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt"><br>
</div>
<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-RIGHT:
0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt"><font style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New 
Roman; DISPLAY: inline">The Company intends to file as soon as practicable 
restated financial information (the &#8220;Restatement&#8221;) in the 
Company&#8217;s amended reports on Form 10-K and on Form 10-Q, as applicable, 
for the Financial Periods (the &#8220;Amended Reports&#8221;).</font></div>
<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify; DISPLAY: block; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt"><br>
</div>
<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-RIGHT:
0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt"><font style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New 
Roman; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; FONT-STYLE: italic; DISPLAY: 
inline">Background</font></div>
<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify; DISPLAY: block; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt"><br>
</div>
<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-RIGHT:
0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt"><font style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New 
Roman; DISPLAY: inline">The Restatement is required due to an error in not 
recording the accrual of certain mineral option fees due in connection with the 
Company&#8217;s mineral option agreement (the &#8220;Agreement&#8221;). The 
other party to the Agreement, which is the Company&#8217;s majority shareholder,
had agreed in November, 2015 and again in June, 2016 to defer payment of the 
fees, currently to January 18, 2017. The unrecorded amounts due were $123,913 at
December 31, 2015 and $148,901 at March 31, 2016.</font></div>
<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify; DISPLAY: block; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt"><br>
</div>
<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-RIGHT:
