<BR>    </I></B></FONT><FONT SIZE=2><B>AGNICO-EAGLE ACQUIRES ADDITIONAL 5.7% OF 
RIDDARHYTTAN SHARES,<BR>  INCREASING OWNERSHIP TO 96.6%;<BR>  FINAL BID 
EXTENSION TO NOVEMBER 4, 2005    <BR>    </B></FONT></P>
<P><FONT SIZE=2>Toronto,
Canada (October&nbsp;24, 2005)&nbsp;&#151;&nbsp;Agnico-Eagle Mines Limited 
("Agnico-Eagle") announced today that an additional 5,979,599&nbsp;shares of 
Riddarhyttan
Resources AB (publ) ("Riddarhyttan") have been tendered to its offer to acquire 
all the outstanding shares of Riddarhyttan not owned by Agnico-Eagle, 
representing 5.7% of the outstanding shares and
voting rights of Riddarhyttan. Together with the 96,189,032&nbsp;shares already 
owned by Agnico-Eagle, representing 91.0% of the outstanding shares and voting 
rights of Riddarhyttan, Agnico-Eagle
now owns an aggregate of 102,168,631&nbsp;shares, or approximately 96.6% of the 
outstanding shares and voting rights of Riddarhyttan. Settlement of shares 
tendered during the previous extension is
expected to be initiated on, or about, October&nbsp;28, 2005. </FONT></P>
<P><FONT SIZE=2>To
enable those shareholders who have not yet tendered to participate in the offer,
the offer has been extended a final time to 4:00&nbsp;p.m. CET (10:00&nbsp;a.m. 
EDT) on November&nbsp;4, 2005.
Settlement of Riddarhyttan shares tendered during the new extension of the offer
period is expected to be initiated on, or about, November&nbsp;11, 2005. 
</FONT></P>
<P><FONT SIZE=2>Following
completion of the offer, Agnico-Eagle intends to initiate the compulsory 
acquisition process under Swedish law to purchase the remaining shares in 
Riddarhyttan and in connection therewith
the shares of Riddarhyttan will be delisted from the Stockholm Stock Exchange. 
</FONT></P>
<P><FONT SIZE=2>Riddarhyttan
shareholders who have questions about the offer should contact Enskilda 
Securities, Nybrokajen 5, 103 36&nbsp;Stockholm, Sweden, +46 8 52 22 95 
00&nbsp;or SEB, Issues&nbsp;&amp;
Part-ownership Programmes, Rissneleden 110, 106 40&nbsp;Stockholm, Sweden, +46 8
639 2750. </FONT></P>
