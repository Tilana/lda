Hearings: 13th to 16th February, 2012
Summing Up: 17th February, 2012
Judgment: 17th February, 2012

JUDGMENT

1.      
The assessors have returned with a unanimous opinion that Viliame Ratoa is guilty of the manslaughter of Kinisimere Wati, contrary to section 239(a)(b)(c)(ii) of the Crimes Decree 2009.

2.      
Obviously, the assessors have accepted the prosecution's version of events 

3.      
I have reviewed the evidence called in this trial and I have directed myself in accordance with the Summing Up I gave the assessors today. The opinions of the three assessors were not perverse. It was open to them to reach such conclusion on the evidence. I accept their opinions and I find the accused guilty as charged and I convict him accordingly.

Salesi Temo
JUDGE
