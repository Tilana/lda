<P align=center><B>INFORMATION STATEMENT</B></P>
<P align=center><B>WE ARE NOT ASKING YOU FOR A PROXY AND <BR>YOU ARE REQUESTED

<P align=center><B>GENERAL</B></P>
<P align=justify>This Information Statement is being furnished to the 
stockholders
  of Merritt Ventures Corp., a Nevada corporation (the &#147;Company&#148; or
  &#147;Merritt&#148;), in connection with the following proposed corporate 
action
  to be taken by the written consent of stockholders holding a majority of the
  voting power of the Company:</P>
<blockquote>
  <blockquote>
    <p align="justify">To amend the Company&#146;s Articles of Incorporation to
      change the name of the Company from &#147;Merritt Ventures Corp.&#148; to
      &#147;Ireland Inc.&#148; (the &#147;Name Change&#148;).</p>
  </blockquote>
</blockquote>
<P align=justify>On November 14, 2005, the Company&#146;s board of directors
unanimously recommended that the Company&#146;s stockholders approve a proposal 
to
effect the Name Change. On November 14, 2005, stockholders holding 54.7% of the
voting power of the Company executed a written consent authorizing and approving
the Name Change.</P>
<P align=justify>The Name Change will become effective upon the filing of the
Certificate of Amendment to the Articles of Incorporation with the Nevada
Secretary of State (the &#147;Certificate of Amendment&#148;). The Company 
anticipates
that the Certificate of Amendment will be filed on or about the 19<SUP>th</SUP>
day of December, 2005 (the &#147;Effective Date&#148;). The proposed Certificate
of
Amendment is attached as Exhibit &#147;A&#148; hereto. </P>
<P align=justify>The date on which the Company first intends to send this
Information Statement to its stockholders is on or around November 28, 2005 (the
&#147;Mailing Date&#148;). No further action is required on the part of any 
other
stockholders of the Company to approve the Name Change. Stockholders who did not
consent to the Name Change are not entitled to dissenters&#146; rights under 
Nevada
law. </P>
<P align=justify>The elimination of the need for a special meeting of the
stockholders of the Company to approve the Name Change is authorized by Section
78.320(2) of the Nevada Revised Statutes (the &#147;NRS&#148;) which provides 
that the
written consent of stockholders holding at least a majority of the voting power
may be substituted for such a special meeting. Pursuant to NRS Section
78.390(1)(b), a majority of the voting power is required in order to approve the
Name Change. In order to eliminate the costs and management time involved in
holding a special meeting and in order to effect the Name Change as early as
possible to accomplish the purposes of the Company as hereafter described, the
Board of Directors of the Company voted to proceed with the Name Change by
obtaining the written consent of stockholders holding a majority of the voting
power of the Company. </P>
