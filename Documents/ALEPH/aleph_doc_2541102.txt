<P style="margin:0px; font-family:Verdana,Times New Roman"><B>Pacific Booker 
Minerals Inc. Announces Board Resignation and Appointment</B></P>
<P style="margin:0px"><BR></P>
<P style="margin:0px; font-family:Verdana,Times New Roman; font-size:9pt" 
align=justify>Vancouver BC, &nbsp;July 22, 2015: &nbsp;Pacific Booker Minerals 
Inc. is pleased to announce the appointment of Victor Eng to it's Board of 
Directors. &nbsp;He will be replacing Mark Gulbrandson, who has resigned from 
the board.</P>
<P style="margin:0px" align=justify><BR></P>
<P style="margin:0px; font-family:Verdana,Times New Roman; font-size:9pt" 
align=justify>The Company would like to thank Mr. Gulbrandson for his 
contribution to the board and for his support of many years.</P>
<P style="margin:0px" align=justify><BR></P>
<P style="margin:0px; font-family:Verdana,Times New Roman; font-size:9pt" 
align=justify>Mr. Eng is a Registered Professional Forest Technician with over 
28 years experience in contract implementation and is currently employed as a 
contract implementation consultant. &nbsp;He began his technician experience 
with the BC Forest Service in Hazelton, BC and later moved to Vernon to work for
Riverside Forest Products Ltd. (now Tolko Industries).</P>
