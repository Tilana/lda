About us:
Zanaga Iron Ore Company Limited (AIM ticker: ZIOC) is the owner of 50% less one share in the
Zanaga Iron Ore Project based in the Republic of Congo (Congo Brazzaville) through its joint
venture partnership with Glencore. The Zanaga Iron Ore Project is one of the largest iron ore
deposits in Africa and has the potential to become a world-class iron ore producer.
