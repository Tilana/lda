<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Arial" style="font-
size:11.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Calgary, Alberta (Sept. 8, 2016) </font></b><font
size="2" face="Arial" style="font-size:11.0pt;">&#150;</font><font size="2" 
face="Arial" style="font-size:11.0pt;"> Suncor announced today that it will 
issue an aggregate of $1.0 billion of senior unsecured Series&nbsp;5 Medium Term
Notes (the &#147;Offering&#148;).&#160; The Offering will be conducted in two 
tranches consisting of $700 million of senior unsecured Series&nbsp;5 Medium 
Term Notes maturing on Sept. 14, 2026 (the &#147;2026 Notes) and $300 million of
senior unsecured Series&nbsp;5 Medium Term Notes maturing on Sept. 13, 2046 (the
&#147;2046 Notes&#148;, and collectively with the 2026 Notes, the 
&#147;Notes&#148;). The 2026 Notes will have a coupon of 3.00% and have been 
priced at $99.751 per note to yield 3.029% and the 2046 Notes will have a coupon
of 4.34% and have been priced at $99.900 per note to yield 4.346%.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Arial" style="font-
size:11.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Arial" style="font-
size:11.0pt;">Suncor intends to use the net proceeds from the sale of the Notes 
to repay existing short-term indebtedness.&#160; </font><font size="2" 
face="Arial" style="font-size:11.0pt;">The Notes are being offered through a 
syndicate of dealers led by CIBC World Markets Inc., BMO Nesbitt Burns Inc. and 
HSBC Securities (Canada) Inc. under Suncor&#146;s short form base shelf 
prospectus dated June&nbsp;29, 2016. The Offering is expected to close on 
Sept.13, 2016.</font></p>
